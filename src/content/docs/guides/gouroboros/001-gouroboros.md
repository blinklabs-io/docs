![gOuroboros-logo](/gOuroboros-logo.png)

gOuroboros is a powerful and versatile framework for building Go apps that interact with the Cardano blockchain. Quickly and easily write Go apps that communicate with Cardano nodes or manage blocks/transactions. Sync the blockchain from a local or remote node, query a local node for protocol parameters or UTxOs by address, and much more.

Byron remains the legacy standalone era, and the Shelley lineage runs through Shelley, Allegra, Mary, Alonzo, Babbage, Conway, and Dijkstra. Dijkstra is the newest supported ledger era in gOuroboros.

Leios does not appear here as a separate terminal ledger era. Current Leios work ships within or on top of Dijkstra, even though experimental `CIP-0164` mini protocol names such as `LeiosFetch`, `LeiosNotify`, and `LeiosVotes` still appear in the API.
